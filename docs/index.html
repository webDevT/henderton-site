Henderton is a growth advisory firm which provides strategic and financial advice to growth companies and their investors.

Supporting tomorrow’s
leaders

Established in 2006, our advisors have been involved in more than 50 transactions over the past 15 years, gaining a reputation for building the best possible equity story for our clients to maximise value and overall deal terms.
This is a record we are proud of and want to maintain, hence our focus on selecting only those companies displaying the characteristics which mean we would be happy investing in them ourselves: good product-market fit, high-potential market sector, strong team, positioning and competitive advantages, business model, and demonstrated traction, among others.
Many of our most successful transactions have come as a result of trusted relationships built up over many years, and our focus and knowledge of key emerging sectors.
