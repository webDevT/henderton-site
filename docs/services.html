Services

Companies come to us for advice at all stages of their business, from strategic planning to fundraising, advice on how to scale, searching for acquisitions, or selling and exiting. Whatever your aims are, we are proud of our flexibility and dedication to helping you succeed.

01
Growth equity

Additional funds can allow innovative founders realise their ambitions and the potential of their vision.

Henderton has a strong track record of helping entrepreneurs and management teams in technology and emerging sectors raise capital, generally focusing on early to expansion stage where we have particular expertise in articulating compelling growth stories and creating best-in-class investor materials.

To do this, we have close relationships with a wide diversity of investors ranging from venture capital funds, private equity, family offices and strategic buyers seeking to invest, buy or partner, and we support them in identifying and executing the most promising strategic opportunities

02
Mergers and acquisitions

An exit is not just a financial transaction – maximising the value of your life’s work is as much an art as a science. It requires careful selection and qualification of potential targets, articulation of the equity story and fit to strategic buyers, and quantification of synergies or joint value to create a competitive situation between buyers. When the time comes, we’ll be by your side, ready to scour the globe to find you the right buyer.

We also work on the buy-side with financial or strategic buyers turning to M&A to acquire new capabilities or technology, round out a product or geographic range, or simply achieve scale. Acquiring the right company today is a great way to position yourself for future growth. Our international experience on both the buy-side and the sell-side gives us the added perspective and understanding to negotiate complex, cross-border deals.

03
Transaction advisory

Henderton offers a range of services to fast-growing companies which are not yet ready to commit to a large-scale transaction such as a fundraising round or outright sale.

This can range from providing clarity to the strategic direction of the company to identification of financial and operational improvement points to optimise shareholder value.

Services include market and sector sizing and analysis, product and market fit assessment, business development and sales support, preparation of investor materials including pitch deck and business plan, valuation analysis, partner or investor research, and strategic board advice.

04
Investor services

Equally, we offer a range of services to investors which do not fall under our full scope M&A services. Investor services include support of portfolio companies with fundraising, valuation, financial modelling, M&A and exit opportunities and secondary transactions.

We are experienced in supporting businesses’ M&A strategies with market mapping and bolt-on strategy – focusing on identifying targets with complementary capabilities to acquire technology or expand service offerings.

Henderton differs from many corporate finance firms in that it benefits from staff with extensive experience gained on the investment side, either in venture capital or sourcing start-ups for corporates, allowing us to understand you and your business better.
