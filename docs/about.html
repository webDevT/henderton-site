About us

Henderton acts as independent, trusted advisors to innovative companies throughout their lifecycle, from raising private capital for fast-growing disrupters and founder-led businesses to cross-border M&A deals.

Entrepreneurial partner and
trusted advisor

Henderton’s international team comes from a range of backgrounds, including venture capital, financial services, management consultancy, and specialist boutiques.
Our team works seamlessly together thanks to a unique methodology and culture based on collaboration and knowledge sharing.
We combine cross-border and sector expertise with an in-depth understanding of clients from start-ups to multinationals.
With many former entrepreneurs and industry executives on our extended team, we understand the unique challenges of scaling a fast-growing business.
We are flexible and can work with third-party investment banking and legal advisors.
“Telenabler was their first Danish customer. I never had much belief we would develop accordingly to our potential if I only talked to Danish VCs, while our potential is clearly international. I was proved right when I met David Jouarisse and he described the map of Venture Capital in Europe, and how we could work to get selected. I understood his methodology was very demanding but also maximised our chance of fulfilling our fast growth potential. Formerly we had dealt with a senior big four financier who admitted that he could never model this complexity. I have seen David’s broad and qualitative network among the European investor community,
in which we identified those fitting us the best. David always keeps going the distance. Eventually this financing round has been a turning point for us, enabling the acquisition of a competitor, international growth and new perspectives. Henderton is to be high listed for fast-growth Scandinavian companies seeking international investors.”

CEO, Telenabler
